Fork & Fire

Our Story

At Fork & Fire, we believe that dining is more than just food on a plate. It's an experience, a journey of flavors that sparks conversation and builds connections. Born in the heart of Norwalk, our restaurant was created by two passionate chefs who sought to bring innovation and authenticity to the table. We source the freshest local ingredients and blend them with culinary artistry to deliver dishes that not only satisfy the palate but also the soul.

Our commitment to excellence drives everything we do, from the carefully crafted meals to the welcoming atmosphere. Every visit is designed to be a memorable one, and we take pride in offering a space where the community can come together to share great food and even better stories. Fork & Fire is where tradition meets innovation.
